About the World Cup

Facts and Figures

The 19 FIFA World Cup tournaments have been won by eight different national teams. Brazil have won five times, and they are the only team to have played in every tournament. The other winners are Italy, with four titles; Germany, with three wins; Argentina and inaugural winners Uruguay, with two; and England, France, and Spain, with one title each. The next three World Cups will be hosted by Brazil in 2014, Russia in 2018, and Qatar in 2022. The USA's best finish at a World Cup was third in 1930. The first ever World Cup was held in Uruguay in 1930 to mark the country's centennial. The scheduled World Cups of 1942 and 1946 were canceled due to the outbreak of World War II. Ever ever since then World Cup matches have provided a steady stream of memorable moments and brought the world together.
